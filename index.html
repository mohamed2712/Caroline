What is Hydrafacial ?

The HydraFacial is a much-loved rejuvenation treatment, using patented Vortex technology to deliver botanical nutrients directly to the skin. HydraFacials infuse skin with healthy doses of hyaluronic acid, red algae extract, copper, zinc, and magnesium peptides to plump and rejuvenate your appearance, and leave you glowing from the inside out. HydraFacials are a miracle treatment for common skin concerns such as: acne, hyperpigmentation, fine lines, wrinkles dryness, redness, inflammation, age spots and can even general dullness. If you are looking to replenish the building blocks which lead to long-lasting, healthy-looking skin then a Hydrafacial could be exactly the ticket.

What does Coctail makeup include ?

Cocktail makeup typically includes a combination of different makeup products, such as foundation, concealer, blush, highlighter, eyeshadow, eyeliner, mascara, and lipstick. The idea is to create a unique and personalized look by mixing and matching different products to achieve the desired result.

Should I wash my hair before doing a haircut ?

It is generally recommended to wash your hair before getting a haircut, as clean hair is easier to work with and can help the stylist achieve the desired style. However, if you prefer to come in with dirty hair, your stylist can still work with it, but it may be more difficult to achieve the desired result.

Can I have a haircut after washing ?
